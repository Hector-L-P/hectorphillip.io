About Us

About La Cocina Venezolana

At La Cocina Venezolana, we bring the authentic taste of Venezuela to your table with every bite. Our mission is to offer high-quality, delicious Venezuelan food that is prepared with love, passion, and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our History

Founded by a group of food enthusiasts, La Cocina Venezolana began as a small family-owned venture with a deep love for Venezuela’s rich culinary culture. We started with a single goal, to bring the best of Venezuela’s flavors to the world, blending traditional dishes like empanadas, arepas, and delicious baked goods with a modern twist.

Opening our doors, we've grown into a brand loved by many, offering an array of delightful treats that evoke the warmth and hospitality of Venezuela.

From our humble beginnings in Trinidad and Tobago, we have expanded our offerings to include a variety of baked goods, savory dishes, and even a few surprises to satisfy your cravings. Every dish is carefully crafted, using the finest ingredients, and prepared fresh to ensure that you always get the best experience.
